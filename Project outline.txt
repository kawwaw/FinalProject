
The project will be dealing with the educational system in Denmark. We will work with a dataset about the educational status of young people in Denmark (15-29 years old). 

Our visualisations will illustrate: 

How are the populations of young people passing through the educational system? 
Is the pattern chancing over the years 2006-2017?
Is there differences between the geographic regions?

The dataset is the tsv-file HFUDD20.txt
In addition there is a geojson file of the regions og Denmark





Reference to the geojson file with danish regions.

https://github.com/ok-dk/dagi/blob/master/geojson/regioner.geojson


The dataset is from Statistics Denmark.  StatBank Denmark table HFUDD20 January 2018.
